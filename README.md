# Mobile Sales Data Power BI

## Overview
Interactive mobile sales dashboard built in Power BI to analyze revenue (769M), transactions (4K), and quantity sold (19K). The dashboard visualizes sales trends by month, city, brand, and day name. It also includes payment method insights, customer ratings, and interactive filters for deeper analysis. Designed to uncover sales patterns and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Mobile retail businesses generate large volumes of sales data across different cities, brands, and payment methods. This project aims to:

- Identify top-performing mobile brands
- Analyze monthly and daily sales trends
- Understand customer payment preferences
- Track city-wise sales performance
- Evaluate customer ratings and buying patterns
- Support business decisions through interactive visual analysis

## Research Questions & Key Findings

1. Highest Sales Month: July recorded the highest mobile sales performance.
2. Lowest Sales Month: February had the lowest sales among all months.
3. Top Performing Brand: Apple generated the highest overall sales.
4. Top Sales City: Delhi achieved the highest sales contribution.
5. Preferred Payment Method: UPI was the most used payment method by customers.
6. Highest Sales Day: Saturday recorded the highest sales volume.
